English Tutoring

I have been volunteering online with UCAN (Uniting Children Across Nations) teach underprivileged students in Colombia English as a second language. This includes English speaking and grammar. Since I have learned conversational Spanish, I am able to communicate with the students and teach them how to properly speak English.

These group settings create a supportive environment and encourage everyone to succeed together.
